about help-for-better-tommorrow

Help-for-better-tommorrow is a non government organisation operating in uganda bukova Iganga along Jinja road which started in August 2019
it is a community based organisation with 115 helpless children from remote families others were got from streets the number of orphans and vulnerable children living in Uganda has been escalating at an alarming rate
we want to change lives of these children some of those children have lost their parents to HIV/AIDS-related complications, wars in our country and suffer the following problems that challenged us to start up a stretching hand to the orphans, vulnerable and needy children in Uganda.
Help-for-better-tommorrow cares for children and vulnerable persons of the community and disabled.
The chidren will be reunited.
One of us has a grand mum who is still alive and she stays with these children in bukova.
We are building our great report with local police and village local leaders who bring the worst orphan cases to authorities any child who has been abandoned,injured or is sick is brought to the center from whence we gladly take them in.
